ScotSwot

Welcome to ScotSwot!

Think you know Scottish lingo? Take the ScotSwot quiz and put your knowledge of uniquely Scottish words and phrases to the test.

How to play?

  1. Click on the “start quiz” button to begin.
  2. Select your preferred difficulty level - easy, medium or hard.
  3. You will be presented with a range of questions, simply pick your chosen answer and hit “submit answer!”.
  4. If your answer is correct, click "next question” or you can try again.
  5. Find out your final score at the end!
